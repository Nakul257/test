Projects

Project Logistics/Project Cargo can be defined as the transportation of large, heavy, high value or a critical piece of material or equipment intended specifically for a given project. At Atromark we believe in the philosophy any weight or any value is critical to a projects

PROJECT(s) and related PROJECT CARGO logistics and transport requirements are truly global in scope and MULTI-MODAL. To enable us to provide a service to meet these requirements we break down the planning, logistics and transport into essential elements, and then assign dedicated key personnel to each of them.

When it comes to Project Management Atromark provide a total logistics solution, which consists of experienced and dedicated personnel, committed to researching all options and resolving any problems should they arise.

Our comprehensive Project Management Services provide the following:

  • Project planning, coordination and execution
  • Project cargo/heavy lift: offshore oil rig supplies, industrial turbines, transformers, oversized equipment and other voluminous steel structures
  • Dry bulk cargo: part and full charters basis
  • Iron and steel: sheets, bars, beams, coils and structures
  • Full/part charter party’s (air charters, time/voyage charters)
  • Special equipment transport and handling
  • Break bulk / Ro~Ro Service
  • Barge / Landing Craft services
  • Cargo Supervision during loading and unloading, as well as in transit
  • Consolidation, Packing and Containerization
  • Feasibility Studies/Route Surveys
  • Cargo Tracking
  • Trucking assistance / service
  • Transport Insurance
  • Dangerous goods handling
  • Industrial packing
